Data Deletion Instructions for RailShot TV

If you would like to delete your data associated with RailShot TV, please follow these steps:

Option 1: Disconnect Facebook Account

  1. Go to Facebook Settings
  2. Find "RailShot TV" in your connected apps
  3. Click "Remove"
  4. This will immediately revoke RailShot TV's access to your Facebook account

Option 2: Uninstall the App

  1. Delete the RailShot TV app from your device
  2. All locally stored data (stream settings, destinations) will be deleted

Option 3: Request Data Deletion

If you want us to delete any data stored on our servers:

Send an email to: jbox38821@gmail.com

Include the following information:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request - RailShot TV"
  • Your name
  • The Facebook account email you used (if applicable)
  • Any other identifying information

We will respond within 48 hours and delete your data within 30 days.

What Data We Delete

When you request deletion, we will remove:

  • Your Facebook access tokens from our database
  • Any stored authentication credentials
  • Your usage data and analytics

What We Cannot Delete

  • Data already sent to third-party platforms (Facebook, YouTube) must be deleted through those platforms directly
  • App Store/Play Store purchase records (managed by Apple/Google)

Questions?

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us at jbox38821@gmail.com

Data Deletion Timeline: We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving it.

Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation once your data has been deleted.
