Privacy Policy for RailShot TV

Last Updated: November 5, 2025

Introduction

RailShot TV ("we", "our", or "us") operates a mobile application for live video streaming. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Facebook Account Information: When you connect your Facebook account, we access:
    • Your public profile information (name, profile picture)
    • Your Facebook Pages that you manage
    • Permission to create live video streams on your behalf

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Stream Data: Information about your live streams (duration, destinations)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Enable you to stream live video to Facebook Pages
  • Manage your stream destinations (YouTube, Facebook, RTMP)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

  • Facebook Access Tokens: Stored securely in Firebase Firestore with encryption
  • Stream Settings: Stored locally on your device
  • No Video Content: We do not store or have access to your video content; it streams directly to your destinations

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Facebook: For authentication and live streaming
  • YouTube: For live streaming (if you choose to connect)
  • Firebase (Google): For backend services and data storage
  • Expo: For app development and delivery

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy:

  • Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/explanation
  • Google/YouTube: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (see Data Deletion section)
  • Disconnect: Revoke app permissions at any time through Facebook settings
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the app at any time

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  1. Disconnect Facebook: Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites → RailShot TV → Remove
  2. Delete App Data: Uninstall the RailShot TV app from your device
  3. Request Data Deletion: Email us at jbox38821@gmail.com with your request

We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

Children's Privacy

RailShot TV is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: jbox38821@gmail.com
  • App Name: RailShot TV

Facebook Data Usage

What data we access from Facebook:

  • Public profile (name, profile picture)
  • List of Pages you manage
  • Ability to create live videos on your Pages

What we do NOT access:

  • Your Facebook posts or photos
  • Your friends list
  • Your private messages
  • Your email address (unless you explicitly provide it)

How long we keep your data:

  • Facebook access tokens: Until you disconnect or they expire
  • Stream settings: Until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • We do not store your video content

Your Consent

By using RailShot TV, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
