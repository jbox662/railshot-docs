Everything You Need to Stream Billiards

Professional streaming tools designed specifically for billiard players

Quick Match Setup

Configure your billiard match in seconds. Set match name, game type (8-Ball, 9-Ball, etc.), player names, and start streaming instantly. No complex configuration required.

  • Instant game configuration
  • Multiple game types supported
  • Player name customization

Perfect Table View

Landscape streaming mode locked for the perfect billiard table view. HD/4K quality captures every shot with professional clarity. Built-in scoreboard overlay keeps viewers engaged.

  • Landscape orientation locked
  • HD/4K streaming quality
  • Live scoreboard overlay

Remote Scoreboard Control

Control the scoreboard from any device using QR code or URL. Perfect for having a dedicated scorekeeper or controlling from multiple angles during your stream.

  • QR code instant connection
  • URL sharing for remote access
  • Multi-device support

Stream to YouTube & Facebook

Connect your YouTube and Facebook accounts directly in the app. Stream to your favorite platforms with just one tap. Manage stream settings, privacy, and titles all from your iPhone.

  • YouTube Live integration
  • Facebook Live support
  • Easy account management

Save Your Best Games

Record your matches locally on your device. Review your games, create highlight reels, and improve your skills by analyzing your gameplay. All recordings saved in high quality.

  • Local recording storage
  • Playback and review
  • High-quality video files

Full Streaming Control

Professional encoder settings, AirPlay/HDMI output support, and complete control over your streaming experience. Customize every aspect to match your setup and preferences.

  • Advanced encoder settings
  • AirPlay/HDMI output
  • Customizable preferences
